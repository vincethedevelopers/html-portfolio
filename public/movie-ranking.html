The Best Movies According to Vince

My top 3 movies of all-time.

James Bond

Watching No Time To Die, I left this world for the duration of the film. I was transported, captivated, charmed, engaged in the characters (especially Daniel Craig's Bond swansong) and emotionally connected with Bond's love affair and the emergence of a gorgeous and endearing Bond legacy. Much was made of the longest running time of any Bond film, but I was so engrossed that I didn't notice the length.

The wolf of wall street

The Wolf of Wall Street is one of my favorite films of all time for many reasons. After seeing it for the first time when I was 13, I was very intrigued with becoming like Jordan Belfort. Now that I am mature enough to know better, and after countless rewatches, I realize that this movie is a critic of this kind of lifestyle. Sure, the drug-fueled, insane, mayhem that Belfort goes through makes for an entertaining movie with an amazing soundtrack. However, I feel that Martin Scorsese intended this to be a display of the way power and money can corrupt someone when he becomes the king of the world and has enough money to spend on several lifetimes.

Crazy asian rich

To start the movie is amazing, highlighting countless up and coming Asian comedians, amazing fashion, and amazing music. It's super close to the book (although it cut out a lot of it to keep it short) and I can't wait to see the other books put into live action. In fact, seeing it on screen surpassed what I could've imagined the dresses and people to look like.
